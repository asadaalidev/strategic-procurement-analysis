##  Project Objectives
- Evaluate procurement performance across **savings, supplier reliability, and compliance**  
- Identify **value leakage** due to delays, defects, and off-contract purchases  
- Benchmark **supplier performance** using KPIs and scorecards  
- Provide **strategic recommendations** for better cost control and supplier management

##  Key Insights
- Procurement achieved **~8% savings**, but nearly **30% of gains were lost** due to quality and compliance gaps.  
- **14.7% maverick spend (~$5.2M)** undermines negotiation power and increases cost leakage.  
- **OTIF (0.54%)** indicates severe reliability risk â€” suppliers often fail to deliver in full or on time.  
- **Delta_Logistics** underperforms in reliability and compliance, while **Gamma_Co** and **Epsilon_Group** consistently outperform.  
- Categories like **MRO** and **Packaging** need tighter control due to higher off-contract activity.
